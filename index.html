About Pathao

Pathao is a cutting-edge ride-sharing app that is revolutionizing the way people commute in cities across Asia. With a user-friendly interface and a reliable network of drivers, Pathao offers affordable and efficient transportation solutions to its users. Whether you need a quick ride to the office, a safe ride home from a late-night party, or a hassle-free ride to run errands around town, Pathao has got you covered.

With a focus on safety and convenience, Pathao ensures that all drivers are thoroughly screened and trained to provide a seamless and secure ride experience. In addition, the app offers a range of innovative features such as real-time tracking, in-app payment options, and ride history tracking to enhance the user experience.

But Pathao is more than just a ride-sharing app. It is a platform that empowers people to create their own livelihoods by becoming drivers and earning money on their own terms. With Pathao, drivers can enjoy flexible working hours, competitive earnings, and the opportunity to be their own boss.

At Pathao, we are committed to making transportation accessible and affordable for everyone. Join us on our mission to create a safer, smarter, and more sustainable future for our cities.
